SVM

The Support Vector Machine (SVM) is a classifier that uses a hyperplane to differentiate between two classes. SVMs minimize the requirement for feature selection due to their ability to generalize effectively in large dimensional feature spaces, making the application of text classification significantly easier. The robustness of SVMs over traditional approaches is another advantage.
